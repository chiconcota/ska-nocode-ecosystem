## 2026-04-27 - Material Symbols Accessibility\n**Learning:** When using Material Symbols ligatures (e.g., `<span className="material-symbols-outlined">star</span>`), screen readers may read the raw ligature text (like "star" or "apps") aloud to users, causing confusion.\n**Action:** Always add `aria-hidden="true"` to decorative Material Symbols spans to hide them from assistive technology.
